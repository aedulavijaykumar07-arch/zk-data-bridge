Not just WhatsApp or Instagram — every application that tries to read your device storage goes through PrivaVault's interception layer. Your original files are locked in a protected vault. Apps only ever see clean, metadata-free isolated copies.

🔒
Original files are always preserved — untouched, forever
When you add a file to PrivaVault, the original is locked in the Secure Vault and never modified. What apps receive is always an isolated clean copy — stripped of metadata, sandboxed, and ephemeral. Even if an app somehow bypasses the shield, your original file remains intact and hidden from it.
🛡️
Universal interception — not a whitelist of known apps
The shield works at the storage permission layer, not per-app. Any process — known, unknown, background, or system — that requests access to vaulted files is intercepted automatically. You don't configure apps. Apps are blocked by default. You grant controlled access per-file, per-request.
